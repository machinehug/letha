Leather

Why leather?

Leather is a durable and flexible material. It lasts a long time if you take care of it. It is breathable and stretches to the size of your feet. It’s also easy to clean.

You’ll have a pair of leather shoes for a very, very long time of you take care of them. And in the end it'll save you money.
